Queues in Bolt, are seperated into 2 types: `Unranked`, and `Ranked`,
Ranked queues being elo based, and Unranked being a casual queue.

## Unranked Queues

Unranked queues are casual queues, where players can play without the pressure of losing elo,
unranked queues allow the option for ping-based matchmaking.

## Ranked Queues

Ranked queues are elo based queues, where players can play to increase their elo, and climb the leaderboards.
Ranked queues also allow the option for ping-based matchmaking, and elo based matchmaking.
