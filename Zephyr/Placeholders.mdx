## Zephyr

These are the all placeholders Zephyr offers.

### Profile
Placeholder                   | Returns                           | Example
----------------------------- | --------------------------------- | --------------------
`%zephyr_profile_kills%`      | The player's kills.               | `273`
`%zephyr_profile_deaths%`     | The player's deaths.              | `24`
`%zephyr_profile_kdr%`        | The player's kills to death ratio | `11.375`
`%zephyr_profile_streak%`     | The player's current kill streak  | `5`
`%zephyr_profile_beststreak%` | The player's best kill streak     | `15`

### Arena

Placeholder            | Returns                             | Example
---------------------- | ----------------------------------- | --------------------
`%zephyr_arena_name%`  | The name of the current arena       | `Flowers`
`%zephyr_arena_timer%` | The seconds until next arena change | `840`
