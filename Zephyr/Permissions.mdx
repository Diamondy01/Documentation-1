A list of all permissions for Zephyr.

Permission                    | Description
----------------------------- | -----------------------------
`zephyr.admin`                | Access to `/zephy reload` and `/zephyr help`
`zephyr.command.build`        | Access to build mode
`zephyr.command.wipeDb`       | Access to wipe the zephyr database
`zephyr.command.wipeProfiles` | Access to wipe zephyr profiles from database
`zephyr.command.wipeInvs`     | Access to wipe inventories from database
`zephyr.command.arena`        | Access to arena commands.
`zephyr.command.kit`          | Access to kit commands.
`zephyr.changearena`          | Access to changing arena.
`zephyr.killeffect.*`         | Access to all kill effects.
`zephyr.killeffect.<effect>`  | Access to a specific kill effect.
