
From commands and features to discord integration, 
each addon integrates seamlessly as a part of Phoenix and extends its functionality.

## Installation

:::warning
Some addons require a paid license to load, they can be purchased on the
[resource page](https://refinedev.xyz/resources/phoenix.3/) under `Child Resources`.
:::

1. In your plugins folder, open Retention's `loader.yml`.
2. For the addon you would like to load, set `ENABLED` to `true`.
3. Reboot your server and the plugin will load.
